# Key Insights

## Sales Trends
- Sales show a noticeable increase toward the later months of the year.
- Quarterly analysis indicates stronger performance in the final quarter.

## Performance Summary
- Total sales and order volume indicate consistent business activity.
- Average order value remains relatively stable across time periods.

## Business Interpretation
- Seasonal trends suggest higher demand toward year-end.
- The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly identify high-performing
  periods and monitor overall sales health.

## Limitations
- Analysis is based on a cleaned dataset with no access to raw data.
- Insights are limited to the fields available in the dataset.
